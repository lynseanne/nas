How to get your API key

You need one free key, one time. Each person uses their own. It takes about 2 minutes.

Gemini Google key — free

This does everything — turns the recording into questions and writes the answers from your notes. Google gives it out free.

  1. On your iPad, open aistudio.google.com/apikey in Safari.
  2. Sign in with your Google account (any Gmail works).
  3. Tap “Create API key”.
  4. If it asks, choose “Create API key in new project”.
  5. Tap Copy. The key starts with AIza…
  6. Come back here → Keys → paste it in the Gemini key box → Save.

Free limit is about 10 lectures a day. Plenty for studying.

Add to your Home Screen

  1. In Safari, tap the Share button (the square with an arrow).
  2. Tap “Add to Home Screen”.
  3. It now opens like a real app, with your keys saved.
